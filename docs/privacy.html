Privacy Policy for FriendZone

Last Updated: November 1, 2024

Introduction

FriendZone ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

  • Real-time location: We collect your GPS coordinates when you're at designated zones
  • Location history: We store your location history for up to 30 days
  • Zone information: We track which zones you've visited and when

Personal Information

  • Phone number: Used for account creation and friend connections
  • Name: Provided during sign-up
  • Apple Sign-In data: Email (if provided) and user ID

Usage Data

  • Friend connections: Who you're connected with
  • Zone memberships: Which zones you've joined or created
  • App interactions: How you use the app features

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Share your location with friends when you're at designated zones
  • Notify friends when you arrive at or leave zones
  • Enable friend connections and zone sharing
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your location with anyone except your approved friends
  • Track your location outside of designated zones (when sharing is set to "Current Zone Only")

We only share your data:

  • With friends you've explicitly connected with
  • When you're at zones you've chosen to share
  • As required by law

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encryption of location data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication via Apple Sign-In
  • Firestore security rules to protect your data
  • Regular security audits

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Control which friends see your location
  • Choose which zones to share
  • Opt out of location sharing at any time

Data Retention

  • Location data: Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Friend connections: Retained until you remove the connection

Age Requirements and Children's Privacy

FriendZone is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Note: While our legal minimum age is 13, the app has a 16+ rating on the App Store due to features including location sharing and social networking. We recommend parental guidance for users under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@friendzone.app
  • Website: https://sidehustleinternational.github.io/friendzone/

By using FriendZone, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
