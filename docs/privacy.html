Overview

FriendZone is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your location data should remain under your control, shared only with people you choose and only in places you designate.

Information We Collect

Location Data: We collect your location only when you're within zones you've created or zones friends have shared with you. Location data is never collected outside of these designated areas.

Contact Information: We store your phone number and email address to enable friend connections and account management.

Friend Connections: We store information about your friend relationships and shared zones to enable the core functionality of the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • Share your location with friends only within zones you've mutually agreed to share
  • Send notifications when friends arrive at shared zones
  • Enable friend discovery through phone numbers
  • Provide customer support when requested

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With friends you've explicitly connected with, and only within shared zones
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (like Firebase for data storage)

Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Create and delete zones at any time
  • Add or remove friends from zones
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Control location sharing permissions through your device settings

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. Your location data is stored securely and accessed only when necessary to provide app functionality.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove all personal data including location history, friend connections, and zones.

Children's Privacy

FriendZone is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "last updated" date.
