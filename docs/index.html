Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add friends?

Use the Add Friends screen to send invitations by phone number. Your friends will receive a notification to join your zones.

How do zones work?

Create zones for places you visit regularly (home, work, gym, etc.). Share them with friends to see when they arrive and get notified when you're both at the same location.

Is my location data private?

Yes! Your location is only shared within zones you create and only with friends you explicitly invite. You can remove friends or delete zones at any time.

Do I need to keep the app open?

No. FriendZone works in the background to notify you when friends arrive at shared zones. You can choose location permissions that work best for you.

Can I turn off notifications?

Absolutely. You can customize notification settings in your device's Settings app or remove yourself from specific zones.

What if I'm having technical issues?

Contact us at support@friendzone.app with details about the issue. We typically respond within 24 hours and are committed to helping you get the most out of FriendZone.
